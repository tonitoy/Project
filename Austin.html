Austin

Brief history

Austin is the capital of Texas and the fourth largest city in Texas. Austin is the state capital of Texas, an inland city bordering the Hill Country region. Home to the University of Texas flagship campus, Austin is known for its eclectic live-music scene centered around country, blues and rock. Its many parks and lakes are popular for hiking, biking, swimming and boating. South of the city, Formula One's Circuit of the Americas raceway has hosted the United States Grand Prix. It was originally founded in 1839 when the first settlers founded a village in Waterloo. It is named after Stephen F. Austin also known as the "Father of Texas". Today Austin is one of most populart tourist destination because of its landmarks and music festivals
