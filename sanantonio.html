San Antonio

Brief History

San Antonio is the 8th largest city in the United States, and the second largest city in Texas. The city was originallyt an Indian village, but in 1718, was officially established as an outpost for the Spanish military. It originally got it's name from Spanish explorers who came upon the location on Saint Anthony's day. San Antonio is now very widely known for it's riverwalk, the Alamo, and the San Antonio Spurs.
