Brief History

The city owes both its name and existence to the university's location along a railroad.The site was chosen as the location for the proposed Agricultural and Mechanical College of Texas, a land-grant school

The population for the Bryan-College Station metropolitan area will range from an estimated 250,846 to 271,773 by 2030.
